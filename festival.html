Fistivals

Moods welcomes all types of celebrations and special occasions—from graduation parties and birthdays to corporate events and family gatherings—whether intimate and small or grand and luxurious. Set in the heart of the city, Moods offers a stylish and modern atmosphere, with custom-designed menus crafted by professional chefs and elegant décor details that create an exceptional experience for every occasion. Moods offers two exclusive event spaces: Aura Room: Perfect for smaller gatherings, comfortably hosting up to 25 guests in a warm and private setting. Luna Room: Designed for larger events, it accommodates up to 120 guests for seated dinners, or up to 200 guests for elegant cocktail receptions, with top-tier service and fully equipped facilities to ensure the success of every event. For reservations and inquiries, contact us via email. moods.resturant.cafe@gmail.com
