To Get Here

Moods Restaurant is located in the heart of Qena city, specifically on 23 July Street behindthe Police Club.The place is easy to reach whether you're driving or walking, and it's near key landmarks.If you're coming from Al-Saa'a Square, head towards the Police Club and you'll find the restaurant on your left.The sign is clear, and the restaurant stands out with its modern interior and exterior design. It's spacious and comfortable, perfect for families and groups, with both indoor and outdoor seating areas. The surrounding area is safe and lively, especially in the evenings. Parking is available nearby, making it convenient for guests arriving by car. The restaurant is also accessible for individuals with special needs. Nearby shops and cafes create a welcoming atmosphere. Whether it's your first visit or you're a regular, getting to Moods is always hassle-free.
